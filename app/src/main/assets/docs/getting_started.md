# Getting Started with ExtroPOS v2

Welcome! Follow these steps to set up your POS.

## 1. Initial Setup
When you first open the app, the **Onboarding Wizard** will guide you:
- **Store Details**: Enter your business name, registration number, and address.
- **Admin Account**: Create a SuperAdmin user with a 4-digit PIN.
- **Activation**: Enter your license key or start a 14-day free trial.

## 2. Opening Your First Shift
Before making sales, you must open a shift:
1. Tap **Shift** in the sidebar.
2. Enter your **Starting Cash** (Floating money).
3. Tap **Open Shift**.

## 3. Making a Sale
1. Tap **Sales** in the sidebar.
2. Select products from the categories.
3. Tap **Pay**.
4. Choose payment method (Cash, Card, DuitNow QR).
5. Print your receipt!

## 4. Closing the Day
At the end of business hours:
1. Go to **Shift Management**.
2. Perform **Shift Closeout**.
3. Count your cash and enter the **Actual Cash** amount.
4. Print your **Z-Report**.
