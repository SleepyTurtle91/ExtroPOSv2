# Multi-Terminal P2P Sync (No-Cloud)

ExtroPOS v2 allows multiple devices to work together on your local WiFi without any cloud subscription fees.

## 1. Roles
- **Backend (Master)**: The central hub that stores all products and receives sales from all counters.
- **Counter (Slave)**: The sales stations that connect to the Backend.

## 2. Setup Procedure
1. **Prepare Master**: 
   - Go to **Settings > Switch Business Mode**.
   - Select **Backend Mode** or **Hybrid Mode**.
   - Tap **Master (Main Station)**.
2. **Prepare Slaves**:
   - On other tablets, go to the same settings.
   - Select **Counter Mode**.
   - Tap **Slave (Counter Extension)**.
3. **Connection**:
   - Ensure all tablets are on the **same WiFi network**.
   - Go to **Settings > Multi-Terminal Sync** on the Slaves to find and connect to the Master.
