# LHDN e-Invoicing (Malaysian Compliance)

ExtroPOS v2 is fully integrated with the LHDN MyInvois system.

## 1. Configuration
Go to **Settings > LHDN MyInvois**:
- **Seller TIN**: Your Tax Identification Number.
- **BRN**: Your Business Registration Number.
- **MSIC Code**: Your 5-digit business activity code.
- **API Credentials**: Obtained from your ERP/POS system registration on the LHDN portal.

## 2. Generating e-Invoices
- **B2C (Retail)**: For standard sales, the app generates a QR code on the receipt for customer verification.
- **Consolidated e-Invoice**: You can group multiple low-value transactions into one daily submission.

## 3. Verifying Submissions
Go to **Settings > LHDN History** to view the status of all submitted documents. A "Valid" status means the transaction is legally recorded with LHDN.
